Kelvin Momo's "Kurhula" Album

South African music producer Kelvin Momo has released his third studio album, "Kurhula", on December, 2023. The album features 27 songs and has total duration of 3 hours and 27 minutes. The title of the album means "peace" and "serenity" in Tsonga language, and fans have praised its therapeutic qualities.

The album's first track, "Kurhula" features Cnethemba Gonelo. The sing is a soulful exploration of Kelvin Momo's unique style and sets for a transcendent musical experience.

Other tracks on the album include "Nothando", a collaboration with Sjava and "Wa Nsiya," featuring Mzizi. The album showcases Kelvin Momo's evolution as an artist and his dedication to expanding the boundaries of Amapiamo.
